Test InfoServiceTwitter

The Twitter service is displayed when you choose Twitter in the Service pop-up menu in the Test Info pane.

This service gets Twitter updates, direct messages, following and followers.

Source: Choose the type of Twitter information to check for with this test. Choices include Twitter updates, direct messages, friends (people you follow), followers (people who follow you), and more.

Other user: Enter the username of another Twitter user. This is only used when following someone else's updates.

In order to know who you are, you should enter your Twitter username and password in the fields at the bottom of the page.

This plug-in was written by Daniel Ellis .
