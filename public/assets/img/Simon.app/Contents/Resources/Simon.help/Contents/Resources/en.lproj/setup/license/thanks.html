License Thanks

This page of the Simon Assistant window appears after you enter your license on the License Editor page. It thanks you for your purchase, and offers some handy buttons so you can recommend Simon to other people. Product reviews and recommendations are very helpful in spreading word of the application. More people using it means more feature suggestions that could benefit you, and more popularity helps justify spending more development time improving it. We really appreciate any kind words you're willing to post about Simon.
