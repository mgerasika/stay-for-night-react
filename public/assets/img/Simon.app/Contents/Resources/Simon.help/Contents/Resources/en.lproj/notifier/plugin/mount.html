Notifier InfoNotifier KindMount plug-in

The Mount plug-in is displayed when you choose Mount in the Notifier Kind pop-up menu in the Notifier Info pane.

This plug-in will mount or dismount a local, AFP, or SMB volume.

Protocol: Choose the protocol for the volume.

On Notification: Choose whether the notification should mount or dismount the volume.

Server: Enter the server name for the AFP or SMB volume.

Volume: Enter the shared volume name.

Username: Enter the username of the volume.

Password: Enter the password for the volume.

This plug-in was written by Daniel Ellis .
